## Problem description

The ingestion service had unstable latency during peak traffic and no clear way to recover from retries.

## Architecture

I redesigned the ingestion layer into a queue-driven architecture with idempotent processors and checkpointing.
The model reduced duplicated jobs and made replay operations predictable.

## Impact

- 67% reduction in p95 ingestion latency
- 40% lower infrastructure cost in the data pipeline
- Zero production incidents related to duplicate event processing for 4 months
