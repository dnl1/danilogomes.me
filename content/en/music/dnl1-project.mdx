This release explores darker pads, controlled low-end movement, and minimal vocal textures. The idea is to stay
dancefloor friendly while still feeling atmospheric.

## Production notes

- Main lead from layered wavetable + analog subtractive patch
- Kick and bass sidechain tuned per section instead of global settings
- Final master targeted for club playback translation first, streaming second
